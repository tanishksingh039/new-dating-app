ShooLuv ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our dating application.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Name and date of birth
  • Email address and phone number
  • Gender and sexual orientation
  • Profile photos and bio
  • University/college information
  • Interests and preferences

Location Data

  • Approximate location (for matching purposes)
  • University geolocation
  • We do NOT track your precise real-time location

Usage Information

  • App interactions (swipes, matches, messages)
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • Log data and analytics
  • Payment transaction data

User-Generated Content

  • Chat messages
  • Photos shared in conversations
  • Voice messages (if applicable)
  • Profile information and updates

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and maintain our dating service
  • Match you with compatible users
  • Process payments and transactions
  • Send notifications about matches and messages
  • Improve app features and user experience
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and violations
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Respond to support requests

3. Information Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal information. We may share your information only in these cases:

With Other Users

  • Your profile information is visible to other users
  • Messages are shared with matched users
  • You control what information appears on your profile

Service Providers

  • Firebase (Google) - Database and authentication
  • Cloud storage providers - Photo storage
  • Analytics providers - App improvement

Legal Requirements

  • To comply with laws and regulations
  • To respond to legal requests
  • To protect rights and safety
  • To prevent fraud and abuse

4. Data Retention

  • Active accounts: Data retained while account is active
  • Deleted accounts: Data deleted within 30 days
  • Legal requirements: Some data may be retained longer if required by law
  • Backups: Deleted data removed from backups within 90 days

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and data
  • Export your data (data portability)
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Withdraw consent at any time

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures:

  • Encryption in transit (HTTPS/SSL)
  • Secure cloud storage (Firebase)

7. Age Requirement

You must be 18 years or older to use ShooLuv. We do not knowingly collect information from users under 18. If we discover a user is under 18, we will immediately delete their account and data.

You can disable location services in your device settings, but this may limit app functionality.
